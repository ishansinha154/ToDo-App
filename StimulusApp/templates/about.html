About this App

Hello! I'm Steemy, generally used by the users to maintain their day-to-day tasks or list everything that they have to do, with the most important task at the top of the list, and the least important task at the bottom. I am pleased to help you in planning your daily schedules. Start winning your goals :)
You can add your Task title and description in the text area and submit it by clicking the submit button. If you want to update or delete any task from your todo list you can press the keys accordingly.
